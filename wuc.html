Wake up caller

Figma / Flutter / Android Studio / Xcode

Overview

This app lets you set up a wake-up call just like at a hotel. You can schedule it to call any phone number at any time you choose.


As an internship, I was responsible for redesigning this existing mobile app (iOS/Android).
The app had not been updated for about four years since its initial release. I aimed to create a more user-friendly interface for existing users while also attracting new users.

Role

- UI/UX designer
- Frontend development

Team

- Me + 2 members
- UI/UX CTO: Business & Backend development
- Customer support

UI improvements

■ Put a CTA in the center

  • Generally, mobile users tend to be goal-oriented, so placing the wider CTA button front and center helps them complete tasks more easily.
  • Most of the existing users are in their 40s to 50s and are not digital natives. Since I changed the CTA’s position, I kept the orange color to make it intuitive for existing users.
