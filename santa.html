UI/UX design

■ Effective Use of Illustrations for a Better UX

  • While mobile apps users are typically goal-oriented, parents with young children are often familiar with colorful, illustration-rich UIs—such as those found in educational games or storybook apps.
  • In this app, setting up a call isn’t just a task. Thoughtfully placed illustrations help evoke the warmth parents feel toward their children.
  • At the same time, for navigation and settings screens, a clean and simple UI is used to support easy and efficient task completion.

■ Reducing Negative Emotion

  • While selecting a present in the app, parents should feel happy—imagining the moment their child receives the call and the gift.

  • However, if the phone number doesn’t work, they may feel disappointed.
  • To ease that negative emotion and keep it from feeling like a serious issue, I use a cute illustration in the error message.
    This helps the user feel more like, “No worries—I'll just try again!”
